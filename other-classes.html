All Programs

Our Specialised Classes

01 · Abacus

Abacus Training

Develop lightning-fast mental calculation skills through our structured Abacus program. Children gain exceptional speed in arithmetic, stronger memory power and improved concentration that benefits them throughout their academic journey.

02 · Vedic Maths

Vedic Mathematics

Teach your child the ancient art of Vedic Maths to solve complex calculations quickly and efficiently. This program builds mathematical confidence, sharpens memory and makes numbers feel easy and enjoyable for every student.

03 · Drawing

Drawing Classes

Nurture your child's creative talents through guided drawing classes covering pencil sketching, colouring and composition. Children learn to express their imagination, improve fine motor skills and develop an eye for detail and beauty.

04 · Bharathanatiyam

Bharathanatiyam

One of India's oldest and most graceful classical dance forms, Bharathanatiyam builds rhythm, body coordination, discipline and cultural pride. Our classes teach authentic adavus, expressions and compositions in a structured and joyful environment.

05 · Phonics

Phonics

Phonics training helps young children crack the code of reading and writing in English. Through systematic sound-letter mapping, children develop strong reading fluency, spelling accuracy and spoken English confidence from an early age.

06 · Hindi Tuition

Hindi Tuition (Spoken Hindi)

Our Hindi Tuition covers both written and spoken Hindi for students of all levels. From basic conversation to grammar and exam preparation, we make Hindi approachable and practical — helping students gain confidence in one of India's most widely spoken languages.

07 · Montessori Training

Montessori Teacher Training

Designed for aspiring early childhood educators, our Montessori Teacher Training program equips participants with the philosophy, materials and practical skills needed to create child-centred learning environments. Led by our founder Poornima Karthik (BBA, MBA, Montessori Trained).

08 · Hand Writing

Hand Writing

Good handwriting builds neatness, discipline and presentation skills. Our handwriting classes focus on letter formation, spacing, posture and pen grip — helping children develop clear, consistent and beautiful handwriting that improves their overall academic performance.

09 · Calligraphy

Calligraphy

Calligraphy is the art of beautiful, expressive writing. Students learn classic letterforms, ink handling and layout composition — a skill that builds patience, focus and artistry. Ideal for children and adults who want to explore creative expression through the written word.
