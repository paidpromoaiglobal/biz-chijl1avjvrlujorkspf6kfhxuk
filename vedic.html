What is Vedic Mathematics?

Vedic Mathematics is a collection of ancient Indian techniques that make complex arithmetic simple and fast. Rooted in 16 sutras (formulas), it helps students solve multiplication, division, squares, cubes and more in their heads — far faster than conventional methods.

At P.K Group School | Academy, our Vedic Maths program is taught in an engaging, step-by-step format suitable for all age groups. Students who practice Vedic Maths consistently show dramatic improvement in exam scores, mental agility, and love for mathematics.
