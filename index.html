Pioneer Kids Play Schools & Academy is a trusted educational institution located at Flat No.7, Seelayam 1st Cross Street, Periyasekkadu, Chennai - 51. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds through focused, personalised teaching across Play School, Day Care, Tuition and a wide range of specialised classes.

🏢 The whole building is premises owned by PK Group — providing a safe, permanent and dedicated space for your child's learning.

Our Academy has been serving the community since 2020, offering Abacus, Vedic Maths, Drawing, Tuition and more. In 2025, we proudly launched Pioneer Kids Play School — welcoming children from Pre-KG, LKG and UKG into our family.
