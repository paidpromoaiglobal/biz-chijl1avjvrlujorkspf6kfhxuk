What is Abacus Training?

Abacus training is a proven method of developing mental arithmetic skills in children using the ancient counting tool — the abacus. At P.K Group School | Academy, our trained instructor guides students from beginner to advanced levels in a structured, fun and interactive manner.

Children who learn Abacus gain exceptional speed in mental calculations, improved concentration, stronger memory power, and enhanced confidence in mathematics — skills that benefit them throughout their academic journey.
