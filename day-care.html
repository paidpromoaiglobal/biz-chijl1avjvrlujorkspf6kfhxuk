At Pioneer Kids Play Schools & Academy, our Day Care program provides a safe, supervised and engaging environment for children between 4 and 14 years of age. We understand that working parents need a reliable and trustworthy place for their children after school hours.

Children are looked after by our trained and caring staff who ensure they complete their homework, enjoy recreational activities and have healthy snacks — all in a structured, home-like atmosphere.
