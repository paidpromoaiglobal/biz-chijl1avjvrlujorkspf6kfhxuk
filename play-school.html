Our Programs

Classes We Offer

Pre-KG

Age: 2.5 to 3.5 Years

Our Pre-KG program is the perfect first step for your toddler. We create a safe, loving and stimulating environment where children explore through sensory play, stories, rhymes and creative activities. Our teachers give individual attention to every child to build confidence and curiosity from day one.

  • Sensory play & exploration activities
  • Rhymes, songs & storytelling
  • Social skills & classroom routine
  • Fine & gross motor skills development

LKG

Age: 3.5 to 4.5 Years

Lower Kindergarten introduces structured learning in a fun and engaging way. Children begin letter recognition, number concepts and basic phonics while developing language and communication skills. Our activity-based curriculum ensures every child enjoys coming to school each day.

  • Alphabet & number recognition
  • Basic phonics & early reading
  • Art, craft & free play
  • Language & communication skills

UKG

Age: 4.5 to 5.5 Years

Upper Kindergarten prepares children for formal schooling with a strong foundation in reading, writing and numeracy. We focus on building independence, problem-solving and a genuine love of learning. By the end of UKG, your child will be confident and fully ready for 1st standard.

  • Reading & early writing skills
  • Number operations & shapes
  • Environmental studies basics
  • Confidence & independence
